The meaning of affordable energy

Most of the times, energy is behind many of our basic human needs, but not only that it is also very useful many other areas of life as you can read at 'Why should affordable and clean energy be important to you'. Foreseeably not everybody can afford such services, "Around 1.6 billion people in developing countries lack access to adequate energy services. 80% live in rural areas"("Access to energy in developing countries"). These rural areas include predominantly sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia.
