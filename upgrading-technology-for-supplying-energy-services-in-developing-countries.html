Upgrade technology for supplying energy services in developing countries.

By 2030, expand infrastructure and upgrade technology for supplying modern and sustainable energy services for all in developing countries, in particular least developed countries, small island developing States, and land-locked developing countries, in accordance with their respective programmes of support.

United Nations website

What does it mean exactly?

By 2030, expand infrastructure in the least developing countries, island or land-locked and improve the technology for supplying energy services to them.

Current progress toward goal

Asia has been expanding access to electricity since 2000 quite rapidly. “The proportion of the global population with access to electricity has increased steadily, from 79 per cent in 2000 to 85 per cent in 2012.”("Progress of Goal 7"). Most of the progress that has been achieved was amongst urban dwellers that created 80% of people that gained access to electricity since 2010.
