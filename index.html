Why should affordable and clean energy be important to you

It was in the late 1870's when Thomas Alva Edison developed and build the first electricity generating power station in New York city ("Pearl Street Station"). Since then the usage of electricity became more and more prominent all the way until 1920' when most of the buisnises and new houses in USA used electric light instead of the previously used gas light. However the electric progress didn't stop with lights, instead electricity started devloping even faster and became important part of people’s lives and soon enough developed into something we now heavily rely everyday.Nevertheless, since in the modern western civilization electricity became so accessible and omnipresent we might be forgeting that “over 1.2 billion people—one in five people of the world’s population—do not have access to electricity”("Ensure access to energy").

There is plethora of reasons for helping to provide electricity services to countries and people without access to electricity in general. Although the most distinctive one being that it is well-developed energy system that powers all sectors, including agriculture, business, medicine, education, infrastructure and more. Without clean and affordable electricity, the quality of all the medical, educational and other sevices the country provides are notably inflicted. Therefore countries without access to this sort of energy accommodation will thrive in all apects of development and further prolong the process of overall improvement.

Indeed, there is a direct relationship between the absence of adequate energy services and many poverty indicators such as infant mortality, illiteracy, life expectancy and total fertility rate.
